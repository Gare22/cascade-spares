ABOUT US

Cascade Spares has proudly served the airline industry for over a decade and is recognized for its expertise by international and domestic carriers around the globe.

We are an accredited distributor and aircraft parts supplier that maintains an extensive inventory of parts from the industry's top manufacturers including Boeing, Airbus, and Bombardier. Our expertise is focused on landing gears, APUs, interiors, slides/rafts, BFE equipment, and key "no go" spares for A320, B737 Classics, B737 NG, B757, B767, B777 and CRJ 700/900 aircraft.

We have acquired and placed multiple 737, 757, & 767 aircrafts into freighter reconfiguration, teardown, and our repurposing programs. Our MRO network is extensive, and we focus our MRO activities with respected and quality-driven providers. Our AOG desk is staffed 24/7 with highly responsive and knowledgeable representatives to quickly and efficiently guide you through your AOG challenge.

A key to our success at Cascade Spares is personalized and customized service that's carefully crafted to your specific needs. By building close relationships with our customers, we can anticipate and respond immediately to your needs. At Cascade Spares, we know you need more than just parts - you need Speed, Reliability, and Results.
